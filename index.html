1 in 8 Women Will Get Breast Cancer in Their Lifetime

Hospitals have a high cost of care with unnecessary diagnostics and treatments, causing patient stress, depression, financial burden, pain, and unnecessary radiation exposure. Patients are forced to take on a passive role in their care.

Introducing PalpAid

Quantified Palpation for
Simplified Health

We are designing a way for women to take on an active role in their health. This hand-held palpation device is quantitative, sensitive, and low-cost.
